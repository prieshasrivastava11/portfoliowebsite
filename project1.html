SOFTWARES USED

Design Concept

The concept of Navrasas, deeply rooted in Indian culture, serves as the foundation of this booklet. These nine fundamental emotions— love (Shringara), laughter (Hasya), compassion (Karuna), anger (Raudra), courage (Veera), fear (Bhayanaka), disgust (Bibhatsa), wonder (Adbhuta), and peace (Shanta) offer a profound exploration into the spectrum of human expression. By selecting Navrasas as the focal point, the booklet aims to shed light on this culturally significant yet often overlooked framework that transcends time and culture, uniting us through shared emotional experiences.

Color Palette and Visual Identity:
Pink was intentionally chosen as the central color palette to encapsulate the tenderness, compassion, and joy inherent in human emotions. The nuanced shades of pink reflect the multifaceted nature of the Navrasas, creating a harmonious visual language that aligns with the delicacy and depth of these emotions. This palette not only symbolizes love and warmth but also acts as a subtle thread weaving the emotions together, enhancing the visual storytelling throughout the booklet.
Typography:
A blend of elegant and modern typefaces emphasizes the timeless relevance of Navrasas, balancing traditional roots with contemporary design aesthetics.
Illustrations and Imagery:
Minimal yet evocative visuals capture the essence of each emotion, allowing the audience to connect with the subtle nuances of human expression.
Layout:
The booklet employs a rhythmic flow, mirroring the ebb and flow of emotions. Each section is designed to immerse readers in the unique energy of each rasa while maintaining coherence within the overall narrative.

This booklet aspires to make the Navrasa accessible and engaging, inviting readers to reflect on their emotional journeys. By celebrating the universality of these timeless emotions, the design encourages a deeper understanding of human connection and self-awareness. In essence, the booklet is not just a collection of pages—it is an experience, a tribute to the richness of Indian culture, and a creative endeavor to spotlight the beauty of emotions that unite us all.
